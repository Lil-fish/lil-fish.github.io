Rest Revolution, 2024

#Experimental Film #Speculative Design

When our body fights back.

In the hypercompetitive societies of East Asia, the relentless demands of work and study have led to a collective neglect of the body's innate need for rest. Ignoring its distress signals, individuals push themselves to extremes, resulting in profound physical and psychological consequences. This project interrogates this crisis through the lens of mind-body dualism, imagining a cybernetic future where mechanized body parts revolt against the brain's oppressive exploitation.

Scene One

Our neck fight against our behaviour of staying up late. It prolongs itself until we cannot see our digital devices anymore.

Scene Two

We grow tails that swipe away our chairs preventing us from sitting all day long.

Scene Three

Our stomach blows a huge balloon, lifting us off the ground when we are emotional eating.
