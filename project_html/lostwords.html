Lost Words, 2023

#Narrative Game #Speculative Design

Imagine a utopia without negative thought by eliminating negative vocabulary.

In a world where cultural prohibition and internet censorship prevail, the regulation of speech remains an ongoing and deeply contentious issue. This speculative video work imagines a future where, in the name of collective well-being, society embraces brainwashing technologies to erase any words from the human mind that might trigger negative thoughts—sorrow, jealousy, pain, or conflict. In this sanitized world, emotional suffering is eliminated at its root. But at what cost?

Main character Alethia(in the name of goddess who seeks truth): wearing the word filter headset.

Chapter One

Alethia enters the underworld—a two-dimensional space where the forbidden words hide among relics. Banished and fragmented, they await rediscovery. Alethia begins to collect scattered letters, seeking to reconstruct the missing language with a sense of loss at heart. Only by reassembling these lost words can the world rebuilt into a third dimensional space.

Chapter Two

Alethia descends via a playground slide into the reconditioning zone—a site of brainwashing where negative words are systematically dissolved and cast into the lower world. Here, emotions are cleansed alongside language, leaving behind only positive shells of thought.
