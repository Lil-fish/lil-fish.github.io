Laughter, 2023

#Interactive Installation #Wearable Design

Collaborated with Shoukang Chen, Shuting Da


This project analyzes participants' inner emotion behind their smile. Wearable devices provide private emotional feedback to individuals, while collective moods are visualized through dynamic projections on the installation. The work explores the insonance between inner feelings and facial expression in social occasion.
