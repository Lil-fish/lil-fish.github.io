Thermo Connection, 2025

#Interactive installation #Creative Computing

This artwork uses temperature as a medium to explore the inner conflict individuals experience during self-discovery: the simultaneous yearning for connection with the external world and the fear of being exposed. Infrared imaging and thermo-sensitive materials make subtle shifts in emotional temperature tangible, exposing the contradictions and tensions within. Yet, when a new participant gently holds the hand, a transformation unfolds. The image gradually calms, with warmth and vitality returning. The residual thermal energy collects one person's warmth and passes it to the next, creating a moment of shared connection.

Components:

- Model: 3D printed hand model + thermo-sensitive painting

- Sensors: Distance(ultrasonic sensor) + Touch(capacitive sensor)

- Interactive Sound & Video: Touchdesigner + Maxmsp + Webcam

Audiences' interactions in pop-up show exhibited in Goldsmiths.
